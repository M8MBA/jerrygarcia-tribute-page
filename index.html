Here's a timeline of Jerry Garcia's life

  • 1942 - Born in San Francisco, California
  • 1946 - two-thirds of garcia's right middle finger was cut off by his brother
  • 1947 - his father died in a fly fishing accident when the family was vacationing near Arcata in Northern California. He slipped after entering the Trinity River, part of the Six Rivers National Forest, and drowned before other fishermen could reach him.
  • 1957 - On his 15th birthday , Jerry recieves an accordian. Disdappointed, he complains until the instrument is exchanged for an electric guitar and amplifier.
  • 1960 - Hoping to see the world Jerry enlists in the US Army at the age of 17, and completes basic training at Fort Ord near Monterey, California.
